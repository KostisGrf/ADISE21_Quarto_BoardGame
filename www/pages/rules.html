Rules

Components

  • A board with 16 squares.
  • 16 diﬀerent pieces each with 4 characteristics: light or dark, round or square, tall or short, solid or hollow.

Object of the Game

  • To establish a line of four pieces, with at least one common characteristic on the board.
  • The line of pieces may be across the board, up and down, or along a diagonal.

Game Play

  • Each player can choose where to place a piece, but the piece he gets to place is chosen by the opponent.
  • The player places the piece on any square on the board

Winning the game

  • A player who creates a line of 4 light pieces or 4 dark pieces or 4 round pieces or 4 square pieces or 4 high pieces or 4 short pieces or 4 solid pieces or 4 hollow pieces.
  • Diﬀerent characteristics can accumulate.

End of the Game

  • Game winner
  • Game even: All the pieces have been placed and no winner declared.
